How It Works - Complete Workflows

üì¶

Post a Load (For Shippers)

How to post your cargo and find carriers

1

Login as Shipper

Sign in with your shipper account or create a new one by selecting "Shipper" role during registration.

2

Click "Post Load"

Navigate to the Post Load page from the dashboard or navigation menu.

3

Enter Load Details

Fill in cargo type, weight, dimensions, pickup location, delivery location, and preferred dates.

4

Set Your Rate

Enter your budget or expected rate in PKR. You can also leave it open for carriers to bid.

5

Add Photos/Documents

Upload images of cargo and any relevant documents for carriers to review.

6

Publish & Receive Bids

Submit your load posting. Carriers will view it and send competitive bids. Review and accept the best offer.

üîç

Find Loads (For Carriers)

How to find cargo and place bids

1

Login as Carrier

Sign in with your carrier account. Make sure your profile and vehicle details are complete.

2

Go to "Find Loads"

Navigate to the Find Loads page to browse all available cargo postings.

3

Filter by Route & Type

Use filters to find loads matching your route, truck type, and preferred cargo categories.

4

View Load Details

Click on a load to see full details including weight, dimensions, pickup/delivery locations, and shipper info.

5

Place Your Bid

Enter your competitive rate and any notes. Submit your bid to the shipper.

6

Get Booking Confirmation

If the shipper accepts your bid, you'll receive a booking confirmation with all trip details.

üöõ

Add a Truck (For Carriers)

How to register your vehicle on the platform

1

Go to Profile ‚Üí My Vehicles

Access your vehicle management section from your profile or dashboard.

2

Click "Add Vehicle"

Start the vehicle registration process by clicking the add button.

3

Enter Vehicle Details

Fill in truck type (Container, Flatbed, Mazda, etc.), capacity, registration number, and model year.

4

Upload Documents

Upload vehicle registration, fitness certificate, and insurance documents for verification.

5

Add Photos

Upload clear photos of your truck from multiple angles to build trust with shippers.

6

Submit for Verification

Submit your vehicle for admin verification. Once approved, it will appear in search results.

üîé

Find Trucks (For Shippers)

How to search for available carriers

1

Go to "Find Trucks"

Navigate to the Find Trucks page from the main menu or dashboard.

2

Set Your Requirements

Filter by truck type, capacity, location, and availability dates.

3

Browse Available Trucks

View list of verified trucks with ratings, photos, and carrier details.

4

Check Carrier Profile

Review carrier's ratings, completed trips, verification status, and reviews from other shippers.

5

Request Quote

Send a quote request to the carrier with your load details and preferred rate.

6

Confirm Booking

Once the carrier responds, negotiate if needed and confirm the booking.
